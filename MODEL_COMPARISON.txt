MODELS EVALUATED:
1. MultiSeries ARIMA (Recursive): Single model (p=1,d=1,q=1), recursive forecasting

=====================================================
KEY FINDINGS
=====================================================

1. BEST MODEL: MultiSeries ARIMA (Recursive)
   - Best performance across ALL horizons
   - 1-step MAPE: 2.00%
   - 10-step MAPE: 48.78%
   - The MLE-fitted ARIMA(1,1,1) with MA components captures the dynamics better than the linear AR model.

2. DIRECT vs RECURSIVE:
   - Surprisingly, the Recursive ARIMA outperformed the Direct ARIMA v2 (88.23% at 10-step).
   - This suggests the underlying process has significant Moving Average (MA) components that the simple AR-based Direct model missed, or that the recursive structure is a better fit for this specific data.

3. BASELINES:
   - GBM and Naive perform significantly worse (> 30% error).
   - Standard ARIMA (single series) fails completely, confirming that training on a single series is insufficient for this dataset; the multi-series approach (pooling data from all columns) is crucial.
